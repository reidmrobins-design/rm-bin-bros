Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first cleaning.

Getting Started

How does the cleaning actually work?
Our truck carries its own water tank and high-pressure, high-temperature wash system. We lift each bin, wash the interior and exterior with jet nozzles, apply an eco-friendly sanitizer, and set it back at your curb — all in a few minutes per bin. All dirty wastewater stays contained in our truck's tank; nothing drains onto your driveway or into storm drains.
Do I need to be home?
Nope. Just leave your bins at the curb (empty, after your regular trash pickup) on your scheduled day and our crew will take care of the rest. You'll get a photo confirmation once the job is done.
What should I do to prepare?
Make sure bins are empty and placed at the curb by 8:00 AM on your service day. Remove any loose items sitting on top of or inside the bin so we can clean it fully.

Scheduling & Booking

How far in advance can I book?
You can book up to 45 days out on our booking page. Time slots are limited per day, so popular days can fill up — especially the day after trash pickup.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes. Email Reid.m.robins@gmail.com or call (512) 740-8076 at least 24 hours before your appointment and we'll move or cancel it at no charge.
Are you open on Sundays?
We're closed Sundays. Our crews run Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Pricing & Payment

How much does it cost?
Pricing starts at $30 per visit for subscription plans and $40 for a one-time clean, covering your first two bins. Extra bins are $5 each. Full details are on our Services & Pricing page.
How and when do I pay?
We collect payment on the day of service — card or cash accepted. Subscription plans are billed monthly; you can cancel anytime with no long-term contract.
What happens if I cancel my subscription after one visit?
Monthly and Bi-Weekly discounted rates apply once you've completed at least two visits. If you cancel after only one visit, that visit is billed at our standard one-time rate ($40) instead of the subscription rate. There's no penalty beyond that — you're never charged for visits you didn't receive.

Service Area & Guarantee

What areas do you serve?
We currently serve the Greater Austin, Texas area and surrounding suburbs. Enter your address at checkout on the booking page and we'll confirm coverage; if we can't reach you yet, we'll let you know.
What if I'm not happy with the clean?
If your bins aren't noticeably cleaner and fresher, tell us within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean them free of charge.

Still have questions?

Reach out any time — we're happy to help.
