About subscription pricing

Monthly and Bi-Weekly rates are discounted for customers who stick with the plan. You can cancel anytime — but if you cancel after just one visit, that visit is billed at our standard one-time rate ($35) instead of the subscription rate. Complete two or more visits and every visit stays at the discounted price.

Not sure which plan fits?

Most households in tight-lid neighborhoods start with the bi-weekly plan. You can always switch later.
